About myself

I am Grippex, a part-time YouTube and Twitch streamer. I started streaming two years ago, the reason I have been doing this is because I like to play a variety of videogames with my friends, and I want to share my passion and love for games to everyone. I really like to interact with my subscribers and viewers, and sometimes I also randomly invite my subscribers to play games together. I actual job is a front-end developer, I write a lot of codes during daytime and brainstorm about new features and ideas for the webpages. But after work, I would spend the whole night playing videogames. I usually stream on weekdays from 7pm to 1am, I would take some breaks on weekend.
